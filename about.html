About Bilacore

Driven by innovation, integrity, and sustainable energy solutions since day one.

Who We Are

Bilacore Petroleums is a trusted energy partner delivering premium fuels and industrial solutions across South Africa. We focus on reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction — ensuring that every litre fuels progress for our clients.

Our foundation is built on decades of experience in the petroleum industry and a passion for cleaner, efficient energy solutions.

Our Journey

2010 — Humble Beginnings

Bilacore was founded with a small fleet and a bold mission — to revolutionize petroleum delivery in local markets.

2015 — Regional Expansion

With growing demand, we expanded our operations across multiple provinces, improving infrastructure and fuel capacity.

2020 — Sustainability Focus

Introduced eco-friendly fuel solutions and efficiency programs aimed at reducing carbon footprint.

2025 — Future Forward

Now serving national clients, we continue investing in technology and sustainability for the future of energy.

What Drives Us Forward
